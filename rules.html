    • Rules

    How to play

    • Pick four (4) any time goal scorers (plus one sub) from the selected Premier League football fixtures listed on our website.
    • Pick all 4 goal scorers correctly and win the Jackpot.
    • If there is no outright winner at the end of the gameweek, the jackpot will rollover to the following Gameweek.

    Using the Player Online Selection Tool

    • Selections must be submitted via The Player's Online Selection Tool (The POST)
    • The Sub Pick can only be used when one of your four initial picks do not feature (zero minutes) in the chosen fixtures.
    • The Wildcard Pick should only be used when a player is not listed in the Team menu. The Wildcard Pick can be found on the The POST at the bottom of the list of teams.

    Goals and scoring

    • If a goal scorer scores multiple goals, only one shall be counted towards the target of four goal scorers.
    • Own goals do not count towards the four goals scorers needed to win the jackpot.

    Cutoff date

    • The deadline is subject to change dependant on the timing of fixtures. We will give fair notification of changes of the deadline via our website.

    Claiming the prize money

    • If more than one player selects all four goal scorers correctly, the jackpot will be shared equally.
    • The total jackpot to be won can be found on our website.
    • Winner(s) will be named within three days and paid within seven days (where possible) of final game of the Gameweek. Payment will be via bank transfer.
    Terms and Conditions

    User Agreement

    • Upon entering the competition, you are agreeing to allow Goals G4lore to publish your name alongside your selections on their website. No other personal information will be published.
    • The Goals G4lore competition is only available to residents of the United Kingdom.
    • £0.50p of the total fee goes towards admin and £1.00 towards the jackpot.
    • 1% of profits will go to our chosen Charity - Prostate Cancer UK.
    • We reserve the right to withhold any jackpot payment(s) indefinitely if there is fair reason to suspect unfair play.
    • In the matter of any disputes, Goals G4lore decision is final.

    GDPR Data Protection

    • Any personal information collected and / or held by Goals G4lore will be treated in accordance with current data protection laws.
